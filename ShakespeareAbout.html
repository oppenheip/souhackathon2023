About this mini website

This website was developed by Priscilla Oppenheimer as part of the Southern Oregon University 2023 hackathon. Her students did much, much better projects than she did. :-)

Priscilla loves Romeo and Juliet and everything related to Shakespeare, which is good because she lives in beautiful Ashland, Oregon, home of the renowned Oregon Shakespeare Festival.

Priscilla created the images for this website using Midjourney and DALL-E2 artificial intelligence. The above image of Juliet is what Priscilla wishes she still looked like. :-)
